About Us


For many years Artur and Saturnina have been working together as professional nature and wildlife filmmakers and photographers.
In addition to the topics of nature and wildlife they also involved in photographing and filming the people (traditional occupations, agriculture, fishery) and monuments.

The results of their work are photographic exhibitions and publications in various journals.
From 2002 to 2005 they worked for "National Geographic Poland". This cooperation resulted in the publications of a number of pictures in both magazines and albums as well as participations in National Geographic exhibitions.

Saturnina graduated from the University of Life Sciences in Poznan (MSc). Artur Homan graduated from the University of Life Sciences in Poznan (MSc). He is also a graduate of the Polish National Film, Television and Theatre Higher School in Lodz (BA) with major in Television Creation and Production in the field of Cinematography, Television and Photography.

Our footage is used by, among others, BBC, Animal Planet, Plimsoll Productions, RTVE, Bibi Film Tv.
